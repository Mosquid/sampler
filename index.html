Audio Sampler

Instructions:

  1. Click "Record" to start recording audio, and click again to stop.
  2. Use the play button to preview your recording and find the perfect sections.
  3. Click and drag on the waveform to select a region - each selection becomes a sample.
  4. Each sample is automatically assigned a keyboard key.
  5. Press the assigned keys to play your samples (you can press multiple keys at once to create "chords").
  6. Drag samples to reposition them within the recording.

Note: Are you sure your keyboard is set to English?
